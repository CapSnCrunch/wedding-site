Attractions

For those traveling from out of town, there are many attractions and activities to enjoy in the area. Here are a few of our favorites:

Bastrop State Park

Explore the "Lost Pines" of Texas with hiking trails, scenic overlooks, and unique loblolly pine forest. Perfect for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts.

McKinney Roughs Nature Park

Over 1,100 acres along the Colorado River offering hiking, mountain biking, kayaking, and bird watching. Stunning river views and diverse wildlife.

Historic Downtown Bastrop

Stroll through charming Main Street with unique shops, antique stores, and historic architecture. Great for an afternoon of browsing and exploring.

Colorado River Activities

Enjoy kayaking, canoeing, or paddleboarding on the scenic Colorado River. Several outfitters offer rentals and guided tours.

Buescher State Park

Just minutes from Smithville, this peaceful park offers fishing, hiking trails, and picnic areas surrounded by beautiful pine and hardwood forests.

Food and Drink

Neighbor's Kitchen & Yard

Bastrop - Craft beer and fire-cooked pizzas with scenic river views and regular live music.

Maxine's Café & Bakery

Bastrop - Renowned for hearty breakfasts and Southern comfort dishes in a cozy setting.

Comfort Café

Smithville - Unique pay-what-you-can café offering delicious comfort food with a mission-driven approach.

Piney Creek Chop House

Bastrop - Upscale dining experience with expertly cooked steaks in a modern, elegant setting.

Smithville Brewing Company

Smithville - Local brewery with craft beers, burgers, pizza, and a welcoming atmosphere.

Store House Market & Eatery

Bastrop - Chef-driven menu featuring locally sourced produce and seasonal specialties.

Franklin Barbecue

Austin - World-famous Texas BBQ. Renowned for incredible brisket (arrive early for the line!).

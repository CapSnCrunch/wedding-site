Attractions

For those traveling from out of town, there are many attractions and activities to enjoy in the area. Here are a few of our favorites:

Bastrop State Park

Bastrop - Explore the "Lost Pines" of Texas with hiking trails, scenic overlooks, and unique loblolly pine forest. Perfect for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts.

Historic Downtown Bastrop

Bastrop - Stroll through charming Main Street with unique shops, antique stores, and historic architecture. Great for an afternoon of browsing and exploring.

Zilker Park

Austin - Austin's beloved 350-acre urban park featuring Barton Springs Pool, beautiful botanical gardens, hiking trails, and stunning views of the downtown skyline.

Pinballz Arcade

Austin - A massive arcade with hundreds of vintage pinball machines, classic arcade games, and modern favorites. Perfect for a fun night out!

South Congress (SoCo)

Austin - Iconic Austin street lined with eclectic boutiques, vintage shops, live music venues, and some of the city's best food trucks and restaurants.

Lady Bird Lake

Austin - Rent a kayak or paddleboard, or stroll the scenic hike-and-bike trail. At sunset, catch the famous Congress Avenue Bridge bat colony emerge!

UT Austin Campus

Austin - Stroll the beautiful 40 Acres, visit the iconic UT Tower, and check out the Blanton Museum of Art. Hook 'em Horns! ü§ò

6th Street & Rainey Street

Austin - Experience Austin's legendary nightlife and live music scene. 6th Street for energetic bars, Rainey Street for laid-back vibes in converted bungalows.

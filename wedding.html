Questions and Answers

What time does the ceremony start?

The ceremony starts at 6:00pm in the evening.

Guests are invited to arrive at 5:30pm to enjoy cocktails and hors d'oeuvres.

I'm from out of town. What hotel should I stay at?

Please refer to the Travel page for accommodation and other local activity recommendations.

Is there a dress code?

Yes, there is - Black Tie Optional! While we highly encourage formal attire, alternative options are welcome. Men are requested to wear tuxedos but may opt for dark suits instead. And while we encourage ladies to wear formal gowns, they're welcome to strut their stuff in cocktail dresses and dressy separates as well! Got a gown or some fancy pants you've been dying to wear but haven't found the right occasion for? This is it!

Can I bring a plus one?

Due to limited space, we cannot guarantee plus ones. Please see your formal invitation for the names of the guests you are invited to bring.

Are children welcome?

To give all our guests the opportunity to celebrate without having to worry about little eyes and ears, we hope you understand our decision to make this a child-free event.

Where should I park?

The venue has ample parking available at the top of the hill for everyone attending.

Is the wedding indoors or outdoors?

The ceremony will be held outdoors on the lawn. The reception will be held indoors with a few outdoor activites avilable on the lawn.

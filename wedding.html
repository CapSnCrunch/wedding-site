What time does the ceremony start?

The ceremony starts at four o' clock in the afternoon.

Is there a dress code?

Yes, there is - Black Tie Optional! While we highly encourage formal attire, alternative options are welcome. Men are requested to wear tuxedos but may opt for dark suits instead. And while we encourage ladies to wear formal gowns, they're welcome to strut their stuff in cocktail dresses and dressy separates as well! Got a gown or some fancy pants you've been dying to wear but haven't found the right occasion for? This is it!

Are children welcome?

To give all our guests the opportunity to celebrate without having to worry about little eyes and ears, we hope you understand our decision to make this a child-free event.

Can I bring a plus one?

Due to limited space, we cannot guarantee a plus one invitation.

I'm from out of town. What hotel should I stay at?

Please refer to "The Weekend" page for all accommodation recommendations. However, you may choose to stay wherever you please! For your convenience, we've also curated a list of our favorite spots/activities in Orange County to help you plan your stay.
